All of our interviews are currently conducted virtually, learn more on how to prepare for your virtual interview. Microsoft may have a COVID-19 workplace vaccination requirement, depending on location, and may require proof of vaccination or an approved accommodation for employees to work onsite. In the U.S., we require proof of vaccination for all employees to enter Microsoft buildings. We have an accommodation process for employees with medical conditions (or other protected reasons such as religion), and anyone granted an accommodation will be required to wear a mask in Microsoft buildings and will need to pass weekly COVID test.
